Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If the product does not meet your expectations, you can request a return or exchange according to the rules below.

1. Return Period

Products can be returned within 14 days from the delivery date.

2. Product Condition

The item must be unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging.

3. Non-returnable Items

Custom-made, damaged by customer, or already assembled products cannot be returned.

4. Refund Process

After inspection, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
